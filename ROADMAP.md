# Project IMACE Roadmap

Project IMACE focuses on building a structured, open, and multidisciplinary research ecosystem for cognitive emulation.

This roadmap outlines the high-level direction of the project.

---

## 1. Foundation Phase

- establish governance, policies, and structure  
- define research scope and conceptual framework  
- build organization-level infrastructure (`.github`)  
- standardize contribution, issue, and citation workflows  

Refer to:
- [Charter](https://github.com/project-imace/charter/blob/main/CHARTER.md)
- [Structure](https://github.com/project-imace/charter/blob/main/STRUCTURE.md)
- [Research Scope](https://github.com/project-imace/charter/blob/main/RESEARCH_SCOPE.md)
- [Licensing](https://github.com/project-imace/charter/blob/main/LICENSING.md)
- [IP Policy](https://github.com/project-imace/charter/blob/main/IP_POLICY.md)

## 2. Research Initialization

- initiate research discussions and proposals  
- develop modular cognitive architecture concepts  
- begin experiment logging and theoretical exploration  
- organize repositories for structured research work

- [Collaboration](https://github.com/project-imace/charter/blob/main/COLLABORATION.md)

## 3. Ecosystem Development

- expand research repositories  
- maintain consistent licensing and citation practices  
- support contributions and collaborative workflows  
- integrate infrastructure, documentation, and research outputs  

Refer to:
- [Contributing](CONTRIBUTING.md)
- [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)

## 4. Long-Term Direction

- develop integrated modular cognitive emulation frameworks  
- produce research artifacts and prototype systems  
- maintain an open, structured, and academically aligned ecosystem

## Summary

Project IMACE progresses through structured foundation, research development, and ecosystem growth, guided by its core governance and policy documents.
